Open Development

Cider is completely open source and free to use outside of the Microsoft Store. We believe users should have a choice and a free look into what they are buying before purchasing something.

Lyrics Support

One key feature missing from most Apple Music clients such as iTunes is lyrics. We prioritized the listening and singing experience with Cider.

Built on Vue.js

Cider is built entirely off of Vue and other open source projects like Electron to provide the best possible experience to all devices.
